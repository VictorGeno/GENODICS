Overall findings

CA84 Evaluation Commentary

  • The Mourvèdre parcel (83,000 vines, 75 rows) shows 2018 pre-treatment mortality around 9%.
  • By 2019, mortality is 5.72% within 0–500 m of the diffuser versus 8.94% at 500–1000 m (≈56% higher in the distant zone).
  • The authors note that results alone are not definitive proof but align with over 300 evaluations across 91 vineyards, where a ~60% mortality reduction is typically observed.
